Course of the Week

Lake Winnipesaukee Golf Club

Course Statistics

  • Holes: 18
  • Par: 72
  • Length: 7,002 yards
  • Slope: 136
  • Rating: 71.3

Detailed Description

Lake Winnipesaukee Golf Club is a stunning golf course nestled in the picturesque Lakes Region of New Hampshire. Situated on the shores of Lake Winnipesaukee in the town of New Durham, this golf club offers golfers an exceptional and challenging golfing experience combined with breathtaking natural beauty. Here's a detailed description of the Lake Winnipesaukee Golf Club:

  • Year Built: 1990
  • Architect(s): Clive Clark, Mark Mungeam
  • Greens: Bent Grass
  • Fairways: Bent Grass
  • Season: Open from May 1st to Closed on November 15th
